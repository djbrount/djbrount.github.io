Get to know me

My name is Daniel, and I am a junior creative writing major at Ball State University. I’ve spent my college career working for various publications and organizations to develop a network and to enhance my creativity.

What I want more than anything is to work with books. Whether I find myself representing them, writing them, publishing them, editing them, or designing them, I know nothing will feel more fulfilling to me than to be a part of that process.

A lot of people call me a workaholic, but I’d say I’m just immersed in my passions. I want to spend my life creating things, and helping make the creativity of others shine.

Sometimes I’ll take breaks to spend hours on random conversation with friends. I’ll relax on the couch watching my current Netflix-binge or playing a video game. I’ll dig through pages of the Internet trying to find that song I heard on the radio or to simply find new music.

But at the end of the day, I return to my love for publishing and creativity. That love of the book never leaves.

To get to know me more, follow me on Twitter, or connect with me on Facebook or LinkedIn.
